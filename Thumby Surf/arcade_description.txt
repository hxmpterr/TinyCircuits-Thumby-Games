--CONTROLS--
A to acelerate
Left/Right to turn
B to use super boost

Thumby Surf is a game where your goal is to try and get as far as you on this infinite map.
There is a persistent high score system and at 10,000 meters, there is a special suprise.
Collect boosts to quickly gain score.
